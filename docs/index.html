Into the Circle

This is an index of recordings made of pipe bands at major and domestic competitions.

Here you can find content by dronechorus, MrUlsterscot, CrazyRed177, Craig Rogers, drumsdotcom, jwramsay16, celticmaps, imBOSS0224, ClanMacRae, rennieaj, LCK217, ThereIsOnlyOneStuart, allynv, samramsaydrummer, pipebandfollower, piperbob2, weekendsinontario, quickmarch, PipesDrumsMagazine, RSPBA HQ, womersleyandrew, tyfry123, StudioStabilo, We Love Pipe Bands/ Loud Pipes Visual Media and TheMillarballs.

I've attempted to automatically classify the band (and solo) recordings by these fantastic video producers, however the process doesn't always get it right. Contact me if you spot an error!
